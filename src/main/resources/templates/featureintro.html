Some Upcoming Features

  • Sample feature as showed in "Test" page

    After logging into the account, there will be a textbox to enter the URL of a YouTube video and take notes on it.

  • Submit notes

    Users have the option to submit the notes and save them to their account.

  • Edit/share notes

    Users have the option to edit the notes and share them with others.

  • Special feature for admin accounts

    Users be able to modify or delete saved notes from standard accounts.
