Tutorial

Click on the "Welcome Guest" dropdown menu to show 3 options:

  • Login: login into an account.
  • Sign Up: sign up for an account.
  • Feedback: provide feedback on your experience with the app.

The left side of the navigation bar contains 4 links other than the tutorial:

  • Home: contains a search bar to find a YouTube video.
  • View Notes: view, edit, and share your saved notes.
  • Upcoming Features: list some features we will implement for iteration 2 and 3.
  • Test: shows a sample page with a search box to embed a YouTube URL and input to write notes.

Most website links are provided at the bottom of the page for convenience.
